Q3 [MEDIUM]: What were the main causes of the 2008 financial crisis?

========================================================================
Question: What were the main causes of the 2008 financial crisis?

The 2008 financial crisis began with an epicenter focused heavily on mortgage-backed securities (MBS) [3]. A major contributing factor was the Wall Street practice of dispersing credit risk among multiple financial institutions [5]. While this practice allowed firms to free up capital and conduct more business by passing risk along, it was accompanied by a wave of lending abuses in the mortgage industry [5]. Ultimately, this system failed because the underlying financial risk still remained within the network [5].

Q4 [HARD]: What is the current state of fusion energy research?

========================================================================
Question: What is the current state of fusion energy research?

Fusion energy research, which has been studied by academic institutions and government laboratories since the 1950s, is divided into two major branches: magnetic confinement fusion (MCF) and inertial confinement fusion (ICF) [4,6]. Additionally, targeted programs like the US ARPA-E ALPHA program have focused on advancing pulsed, intermediate-density fusion approaches—such as magneto-inertial fusion and Z-pinch variants—to develop lower-cost pathways to commercial power [1]. Recent developments and milestones in the field center around reactor advancements, breakthroughs in fusion energy, and achieving net energy gain [5].
